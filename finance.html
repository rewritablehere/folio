Money Log

A simple webapp for tabbing personal earnings and expenditures

#Earnings #Spends

Why?

I somehow don't keep a clear tab of my spending. No app has helped me till now sufficiently. I also tend to procrastinate when it comes to sitting down and identifying what I have earnt, spent and being diligent on my spendings.


The Design

At the most fundamental level, this information needs to reach user as to whether he or she is doing good in terms of their spending vs earning. If not we just inform them the same thing they already know using colour for the total figures. And we give them a list each to look at; one for the earnings and the other for their expenditure. Also what is attempted is categorizing the expenditure by recurring and unique spends.
